GIS for election campaigns and political management
Our GIS software is used for election activities from federal and regional to local levels. It is used for creation of electronic portraits of electoral districts including multiple social-economic and ecological variables, for electoral programs, for the analysis and interpretation results of elections and for forecasting. It is used for the federal level and regional election campaigns since 1995
