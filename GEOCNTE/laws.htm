Laws and regulations for GIS

GIS will play an important role only if the duties to implement GIS and to use GIS will be included into the system of laws, legal regulations and technical norms. Therefore we try to include the respective part to any GIS project.
It includes :

- the investigation of existing documents and extracting all the requirements in creation and use of map/coordinate descriptions in the concrete area; it is essential to investigate all the relations which touch the spatial data infrastructure of the regions/cities;


- building the graphs of all the existing relations in this field


- analysis of required changes in the existing documents which will be necessary to implement in order to get the maximal effects from GIS;


- preparation of the draft of regional laws, legal regulations and technical norms and making test projects before the final signing of the documents.
