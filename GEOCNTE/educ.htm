Education and scientific investigations


Our GIS software is used more than in 50 universities and colleges in Russia and abroad. The Center takes part in the Russian Ministry of education GIS activity to support the system of regional advanced information and technological centers belonging universities. We help them by the software, education, searching for the projects, design GIS technologies, experience etc. We are also closely collaborate with Russian branch of UNIGIS started in 1999.
About 50 academic and research institutes use our GIS software for experimental projects, case studies, applications etc. in wide range of areas.
