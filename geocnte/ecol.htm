GIS application areas

  • Ecology
    In field of ecology GIS are used by the Federal Center of Geoecological systems of the Russian Ministry of Ecology, by regional and municipal ecological committees, by administrations of natural reserves and parks, by state, municipal and private project ecological firms, as well as by respective faculties of universities and research institutes. All mentioned types of organizations use our GIS software GeoDraw / GeoGraph / GeoConstructor for GIS database creation, support and for environmental management. The use of data created by many other organizations is the special feature of ecological activity. Therefore the use of regional and municipal GIS infrastructure is very important in this field. The example of GIS applications like “overlay on fly” see on the map picture (overlay of pollution zones by different components). You need only select the layers to overlay and apply a query (spatial, QBE, SQL query) and the results as a critical zones will be immediately displayed and reported.
