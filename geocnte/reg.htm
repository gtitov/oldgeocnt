GIS application areas

  • Regional administrations
    One of the most promising direction is - to use GIS as the basis for information systems for regional administrations and to manage the territory as a whole using information systems of different organizations and the total regional GIS infrastructure.
    In Komi republic from 1996 we are the main subcontractor in design such a complex cadastral GIS. The problem is solved by Komi cadastral GIS center – unique structure in Russian regions. During this time we have reach the essential success in organizing interactions of different organizations – forest, road, land, geology, ecology, gas, architecture and urban planning committees and firms on the base of regional GIS infrastructure, including update different features as parts of a whole regional systems.
