GIS application areas

  • Urban planning and management
    GIS is used for creation and support of urban GIS infrastructure in many cities of Russia from the largest (Moscow, Sankt-Petersburg) to the small towns (like 10-30 thousands habitants). Application fields include the support in the actual state multi-scale digital maps implementing field survey data created during projecting, constructing and exploiting, cadastral data etc., technologies of interactions between information systems of different city services (utility companies, architecture, land committee, road committee etc.) for the integrated municipal management etc.
    Our GIS software is used in architectural and urban planning committees, information boards of city administrations, for electronic atlases of cities. For the example CD of Krasnoyarsk city with more than 1 mln. population including GIS, digital map with each building and hypertext database costs lesser than US$3.
