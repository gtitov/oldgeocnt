GIS application areas

  • Land use and management
    Land cadastral system is a developing direction in Russia due to the implementation the new relations like “private property for immobility”, “land rent” and “land ownership and property” as one of the most important parts of transaction to the market economy from 1992.
    Our GIS software is used by many regional and land committees for creation of cadastral maps and use it as GIS subsystem of cadastral document system. The special subjects are GIS/GPS intergration and help to design land cadastral systems as a part of regional and municipal GIS infrastructure, as well as the interaction between information systems of land committees and other services on the same territory.
    Our GIS software is also certified by the responsible Federal state center for use for cadastral maps.
    Head of GIS Research Center IGRAS Dr.Kazantsev is an official advisor of deputy chairman of the Russian State Land Committee in GIS.
